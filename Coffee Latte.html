Coffee Latte- Smooth, Mellow & Milky

About

Coffee Latte is smooth and mellow coffee made with a shot of espresso and a generous amount of steamed milk. It has a creamy texture and light coffee flavor, making it perfect for beginners or anyone who enjpoys a gentle coffee experience.

Benefit

Coffee Latte provides a clam energy boost with less caffeine than stronger coffees. The warm milk helps in relaxtion and digestion, making it an ideal choice for morning or eveing comfort.
