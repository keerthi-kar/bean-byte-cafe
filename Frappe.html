Frappe Magic - Sip, Chill, Repeat

About

Frappe is a refreshing iced coffee made by blending instant coffee, sugar, cold water or milk and ice cubes to create a foamy, creamy delight. It's the perfect pick-me-up for hot days, combining coolness with coffee kick.

Benefit

Frappe helps cool down your body while still giving you the energy boost from coffee. It's a great way to stay refreashed, reduce heat fatigue and enjoy your caffeine with a twist.
