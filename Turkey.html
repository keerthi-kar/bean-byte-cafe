About

Turkish Tea is a rich, black tea traditionally brewed in a two-layered teapot and served without milk. It's strong in flavor, slightly bitter and often enjoyed in small tulip-shaped glasses. It represents Turkish culture, hospitality and warmth.

Benefit

Turkish tea is packed with antioxidants, helps digestion and keeps you alert without being too harsh. It's a calming drink that also gives a subtle energy lift, perfect for social moments or quiet breaks.
