Cappuccino - A Classic Coffee Experience

About

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Known for its thick creamy top and rich aroma, it offers a perfect balance of strong and smooth flavors. Ideal for coffee lovers who enjoy a comforting and full-bodied cup.

Benefit

Cappuccino can refresh your mind while calming your mood.The espresso helps increase focus and alertness, while the warm milk adds a relaxing touch. It's also known to improve digestion when consumed in moderation.
