Iced Mocha - When Coffee Falls in Love with Chocolate

About

Iced Mocha is a delicious blend of espresso, cold milk, chocolate syrup and ice. It's sweet, bold and chilled - the perfect drink for those who love the richness of coffee with the sweetness of chocolate in every sip.

Benefit

Iced mocha refreshed you body while uplifting your mood with the combo of caffeiene and chocolate. It gives an instant energy boost and is perfect for satisfying both your coffee and sweet cravings.
