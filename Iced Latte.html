Whispering Chill - The Calm of Iced Latte

About

Iced Latte is a smooth, chilled coffee made with espresso and cold milk, served over ice. It has a light, creamy texture and just the right amount of coffee kich, making it a perfect refreshing drink for warm days.

Benefit

Iced Latte cools you down while gently boosting your energy. It's low on bitterness, easy to digest and ideal for those who prefer a light, relaxing coffee experience.
