Flat White - Smooth & Creamy Delight

About

Flat White ia a velvety-smooth coffee made with a double shot of espresso and steamed milk. It's creamier than a latte and perfect for those who enjoy rich yet balanced flavors.

Benefit

Flat White offers a perfect balance of strong coffee and smooth milk, giving you a rich taste without bitterness. It provides an energy boost from espresso and the calming feel of steamed milk. Ideal for relaxing moments or focused work sessions.
